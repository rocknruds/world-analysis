PowerFlow is a geopolitical intelligence platform that tracks how power actually moves through the world — not as states declare it, but as events reveal it. Built around the idea that every actor's position is contingent on its relationships, it scores states and non-state groups on Authority and Reach, maps the dependencies between them, and traces how disturbances cascade through the system. The analytical engine lives in a private Notion knowledge graph where agents continuously ingest intelligence, update scores, and surface reasoning — and a public-facing Next.js app translates that into actor profiles, score trajectories, and conflict tracking designed to make analysis legible, not just display data. The goal isn't another ranking or dashboard; it's a living record of how influence, control, and geopolitical reality actually shift over time.
