# PowerFlow Color Scheme Reference

## Dark Mode (default)
- Background: #0e1220 (deep slate-navy)
- Surface: #151929
- Surface raised: #1c2135
- Border: #1f2640
- Foreground / primary text: #e8eaf0 (cool off-white)
- Muted text: #6b7499
- Muted foreground: #9099b8
- Accent (primary): #3b82f6 (blue)
- Accent dim: #2563eb

## Light Mode
- Background: #f5f4f0 (warm parchment)
- Surface: #ffffff
- Surface raised: #efede8
- Border: #dedad4
- Foreground / primary text: #1a1918 (near-black warm)
- Muted text: #6b6b68
- Muted foreground: #9a9a96
- Accent (primary): #2563eb (deeper blue)
- Accent dim: #1d4ed8

## Score / Semantic Colors
### Dark mode
- Score high / positive delta: #22c55e (green)
- Score mid / warning: #f59e0b (amber)
- Score low / negative delta: #ef4444 (red)
- PF Score accent: #3b82f6 (blue)
- Authority score: #f59e0b (amber)
- Reach score: #f97316 (orange)

### Light mode
- Score high / positive delta: #16a34a (green, desaturated)
- Score mid / warning: #d97706 (amber, desaturated)
- Score low / negative delta: #dc2626 (red, desaturated)

## Overall Design Language
- Dark mode feel: cool blue-slate intelligence dashboard, clinical and data-dense
- Light mode feel: warm editorial/parchment, readable and grounded
- Both modes share the same blue accent family
- A subtle grid overlay is applied globally (blue-tinted in dark, neutral in light)
- Typography: Funnel Sans (sans-serif) + Playfair Display (serif accent)
- Border radius: 0.375rem (subtle, not pill-shaped)
